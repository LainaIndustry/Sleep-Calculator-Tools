Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2024

1. Information We Collect

At SleepCalculator, we value your privacy. We collect minimal information to provide our free sleep calculator tools:

Information You Provide

  • Sleep times and preferences (when using our calculators)
  • Contact information (if you contact us)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited on our site
  • Time spent on our sleep calculators

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide our sleep calculator tools
  • Improve our website and calculators
  • Respond to your questions
  • Understand how people use our sleep tools

3. Data Protection

We take steps to protect your information:

  • We use secure connections (SSL)
  • We don't store personal sleep data long-term
  • We limit access to your information

4. Third-Party Services

We use these services to help our website work:

Google Analytics

Helps us understand how visitors use our sleep calculators so we can improve them.

Google AdSense

Shows ads to help support our free sleep tools. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what information we have about you
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how our sleep calculators are used
  • Show relevant advertisements

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will post any changes on this page and update the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

Email: privacy@sleepcalculator.com
