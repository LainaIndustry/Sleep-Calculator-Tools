Sleep Calculator FAQs

How does the sleep calculator work?

Our calculator uses sleep science to find the best times to wake up or go to sleep based on 90-minute sleep cycles.

What are sleep cycles?

Sleep cycles are 90-minute periods during sleep. Waking up between cycles helps you feel more rested.

Is this calculator free?

Yes, all our sleep calculators are completely free to use with no sign up required.
