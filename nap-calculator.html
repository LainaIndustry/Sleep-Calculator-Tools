Nap Science Explained

Power Nap (10-20 minutes)

Quick energy boost, improves alertness and performance without grogginess. Perfect for a midday pick-me-up.

90-Minute Nap

Complete sleep cycle nap. Includes all sleep stages, enhances creativity, emotional memory, and procedural memory.

Avoid 30-60 Minute Naps

Waking during deep sleep causes sleep inertia - that groggy, disoriented feeling. Either take short or full cycle naps.
