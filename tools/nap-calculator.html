Nap Calculator FAQs

What's the best time to nap?

The best time is usually 7-8 hours after waking up, but before 3 PM to not affect night sleep.

How long should I nap?

10-20 minutes for quick energy, 90 minutes for complete sleep cycle rest.

Will napping affect my nighttime sleep?

Not if you nap before 3 PM and keep naps short (under 30 minutes).
