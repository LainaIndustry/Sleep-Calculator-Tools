Baby Sleep FAQs

When should I start sleep training?

Most babies are ready for sleep training around 4-6 months old, but check with your pediatrician.

How much sleep does my baby need?

Newborns need 14-17 hours, infants 12-15 hours, toddlers 11-14 hours of total sleep per day.

What if my baby doesn't follow the schedule?

Use the schedule as a guide. Every baby is different and may need adjustments.
