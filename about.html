Our Mission

Sleep Calculator was founded with a simple mission: to help people achieve better sleep through accessible, science-based tools and educational content. We believe that quality sleep is fundamental to health, happiness, and productivity.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a team of sleep enthusiasts and web developers, Sleep Calculator emerged from personal struggles with sleep quality. After discovering the power of sleep cycle calculations, we wanted to create a tool that would make this knowledge accessible to everyone.

The Science Behind Our Calculator

Our sleep calculator is based on established sleep science research. The 90-minute sleep cycle is well-documented in sleep studies, and timing your sleep to complete full cycles can significantly impact how refreshed you feel upon waking.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Providing accurate, science-based sleep tools
  • Creating educational content that empowers better sleep habits
  • Maintaining a free, accessible platform for all users
  • Continuously improving our tools based on user feedback and latest research

Meet Our Team

While we're a small team, we're passionate about sleep health and creating tools that make a real difference in people's lives. Our team includes web developers, content creators, and sleep research enthusiasts.
